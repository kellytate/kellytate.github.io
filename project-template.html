Limn Social Journal

Limn

Social Media Journal App built with Django and PostgreSQL

February 2023
  • Collaborated over the full software development lifecycle, from planning to implementation
  • Integrated journaling app with social media connectivity, visual and audio media, and data analysis and visualization
  • APIs and libraries: Spotify API, LocationIQ, Cloudinary, FullCalendar, amCharts 5, Nano Gallery 2
GitHub Repo

Limn was initially developed and implemented over a period of 10 days during February 2023.

The intention behind Limn is to give users a dedicated space to craft records of memories -- a multimedia digital scrapbook to organize memories in whichever way feels most natural. Limn allows users to upload images, link to videos, add maps of locations, and add tracks and playlists from spotify.
